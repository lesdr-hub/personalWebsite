About

I am an aspiring digital artist with a particular passion for storytelling and worldbuilding. I'm especially skilled in bridging the gap from simple ideas and words on a page to visual reference that beautifully brings the imagined world to life.


As both a writer, storyteller, and artist, I excel in creating living worlds with writing, and transforming them into a visual medium.

THE JOURNEY

Since 2024, a multitude of things have happened in my life to drive me to pursue art as much as I did. Many resources popped up as time went on, as well as many highs and lows. The following timeline is a condensed version of those events.

April 2024
Beginning
I began in early April of 2024 because I was gifted an iPad. I'd always enjoyed doodling, but I only began taking it seriously then- when I realized I was given the resources and an opportunity to learn something.
August 2024
Inspiration
After months of practicing and inefficiently searching for resources on the subject, I began to follow a structured routine of drawing from reference on a regular basis. This significantly helped my progress.
October 2024
First Commission
A friend of mine saw my drawings and asked me if I could draw him. The experience of doing so allowed me to relate the visual world to the art I was creating.
December 2024
Worldbuilding
I'd always loved creating worlds as an avid reader. It felt freeing to be able to build a cohesive universe, to create a narrative, and to make it real. It was late 2024 when I'd felt confident enough in my art to draw my vision for one of the characters I'd written.
March 2025
Storytelling
After a short break from practice, I began to tell stories with my art. Meaningful, intentful stories, that I had intended to express in a drawing. It was here that I felt like I really understood what art means.
April 2025
Cohesiveness
I drew daily throughout the spring of 2025, on a regular basis. Of my friends, of my characters, of the worlds I'd written. I began to really feel like I understood what I wanted to make, and how it connects to what I was drawing.
